

Helter Skelter
---
**переполох, суматоха, беспорядок**

*Disorderly haste or confusion; chaotic rushing about.*

1. The office descended into **helter skelter** when production went down five minutes before the demo.
2. Kids ran **helter skelter** around the playground after the bell rang.
3. It was **helter skelter** backstage in the minutes before the show started.
