

Humpty-Dumpty
---
**Шалтай-Болтай**

*A character from an English nursery rhyme, depicted as a large egg sitting on a wall; used to refer to something that cannot be put back together once broken.*

1. Once the monolith was split apart, there was no putting **Humpty-Dumpty** back together again.
2. Like **Humpty-Dumpty**, the shattered database schema couldn't simply be reassembled.
3. All the king's horses and all the king's men couldn't put **Humpty-Dumpty** together again.
