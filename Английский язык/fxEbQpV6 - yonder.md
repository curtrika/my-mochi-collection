

yonder
---
**вон там, вдалеке (устаревшее/диалектное)**

*Old-fashioned or dialectal word meaning "over there", at a distance from the speaker.*

1. The fresh blooms on **yonder** tree caught her eye.
2. Look **yonder** — that's the old mill on the hill.
3. He pointed **yonder**, towards the village in the distance.
