

prod
---
**подталкивать, тыкать; толчок**

*To push someone or something, often to make them act; also, a quick push or nudge.*

1. A gentle **prod** from the tech lead got the stalled PR merged before the deadline.
2. She gave the frozen service a **prod** by restarting the pod.
3. He needed a **prod** before he finally started writing unit tests.
