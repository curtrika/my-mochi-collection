postpone
---
**откладывать, переносить**

*To delay something until a later time.*

1. The meeting was **postponed** due to the server outage.
2. Don't **postpone** difficult conversations — they only get harder.
3. The release was **postponed** by two weeks after the bug was found.

---
**подглядывать, шпионить, совать нос**

*To secretly look around or investigate someone's private affairs.*

1. The junior dev was caught **snooping** through production logs.
2. Don't **snoop** through someone's files without permission.
3. She felt uneasy knowing someone had been **snooping** around her desk.
