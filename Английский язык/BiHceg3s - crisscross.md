

crisscross
---
**пересекать(ся) крест-накрест, испещрять**

*To move or cross something repeatedly in different directions, forming a pattern of intersecting lines.*

1. Goroutines **crisscrossed** the call graph so many times that the trace was unreadable.
2. Cables **crisscross** the server rack in a chaotic tangle behind the racks.
3. Narrow cobbled streets **crisscross** the old town centre.
