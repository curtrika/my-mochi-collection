

Chick flick
---
**ромком (фильм для девушек)**

*An informal term for a film aimed mainly at a female audience, typically romantic or emotional.*

1. He reluctantly agreed to watch a **chick flick** with her on Friday night.
2. Critics rarely take **chick flicks** seriously, even though they're often huge box-office hits.
3. Notting Hill is considered a classic **chick flick**.
