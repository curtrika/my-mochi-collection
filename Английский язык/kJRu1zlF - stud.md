**жеребец (племенной); заклёпка, шип; стойка (в стене)**

*A male animal kept for breeding; also a small metal knob for decoration, or an upright wall support.*

1. The racehorse was retired to **stud** after a training injury.
2. He used a **stud** finder to locate the wall studs before drilling.
