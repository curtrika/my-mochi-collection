

browbeat
---
**запугивать, стращать, давить на кого-то**

*To force someone into doing something by being aggressive or intimidating.*

1. The tech lead tried to **browbeat** the intern into skipping the code review.
2. Don't let an aggressive PM **browbeat** you into cutting corners on testing.
3. She was **browbeaten** into signing the contract she hadn't fully read.
