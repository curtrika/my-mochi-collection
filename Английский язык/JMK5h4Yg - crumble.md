

crumble
---
**рассыпаться, крошиться, разрушаться**

*To break into small pieces, or to gradually collapse or fail.*

1. Legacy systems tend to **crumble** under the weight of untested changes.
2. The old server room's wiring was starting to **crumble** with age.
3. Apple **crumble** is a classic British dessert.
